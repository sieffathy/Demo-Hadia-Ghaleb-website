ABOUT US

Dubai-based entrepreneur and fashion influencer Hadia Ghaleb launches her inclusive
swimwear line Hadia Ghaleb Label. After years spent observing the growing fashion gap in the swimwear market between veiled and unveiled women, with there even being separate stores
and designs, Ghaleb has innovated an empowering swimwear line for all women which bridges
the swimwear divide for women of all cultures, religions, and lifestyles, bringing much-needed change and inclusivity to the fashion industry.

Having the Arab woman at the heart of the brand, it was an alien notion for Hadia that veiled
and unveiled women are separated by their swimwear styles. And so came the idea of
a "Swimsuit for all (pour toutes)".

Hadia Ghaleb swimsuits are made to provide the UV-protection, quick-dry and luxury that we
all seek when going on vacation, or just relaxing by the pool on a sunny day.

“For our collection, we took inspiration from the elegance of Arab women and the vibrant,
summer colors of days spent at the beach. Just as I have nostalgic memories of summer
vacations in Sahel where I've been going since I was a child, I want the Hadia Ghaleb Label to
give all of our customers the opportunity to create unforgettable memories. Our bright, on-trend
prints and smart design solutions allow women to combine their swimwear items in various
ways to cover up as much or as little as they feel comfortable with.” - Founder, Hadia Ghaleb.
